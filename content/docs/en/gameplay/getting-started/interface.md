# Game Interface

Complete guide to Hytale's user interface. The UI/UX team designed Hytale's interface to provide an immersive, intuitive, and universal experience across all game modes.

## Main Screen (HUD)

The HUD (heads-up display) appears on-screen while playing, superimposed on your view of the game world without obstructing gameplay.

### Health Bar

Located at the **bottom of the screen, above the hotbar**:
- **Red bar** - Your current health (default 100 HP)
- **Regeneration** - Refills based on hunger status
- **Damage** - Bar depletes when taking damage; reaching zero causes death

### Hunger Bar

Also located at the bottom near health:
- **Hunger** - Linked to how well fed your character is
- **Visibility** - Does not appear when the bar is full
- **Health regeneration** - Eating fills this bar and plays a central role in regenerating health

### Mana Bar

For magic weapon users:
- **Blue gauge** - Located on the left side of the hotbar
- **Usage** - Required for magical weapons
- **Regeneration** - Refills over time

### Hotbar

At the bottom center of the screen:
- **9 slots** - Quick access to items and blocks
- **Selection** - Keys 1-9 or scroll wheel
- **Active item** - Currently selected slot is highlighted

### Crosshair

- **Small dot** - Located in the center of the screen
- **Purpose** - Shows the aim point of the item being held

### Directional Indicator

- **Compass** - Located at the top of the screen
- **Direction** - Shows cardinal directions for navigation

### Navigation Icons

Located in the bottom-right of the screen:
- **Map icon** - Opens the world map (M key)
- **Inventory icon** - Opens inventory (Tab key)
- Additional UI element shortcuts

## Inventory

Press **Tab** to open the inventory.

### Main Storage

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Main inventory | Default slots, expandable via backpacks |
| Backpack slot | Located right of your character model |
| Equipment | Equipped armor and gear |
| Pocket crafting | Basic crafting without a workbench |

### Backpack Upgrades

Your inventory can be expanded through backpack upgrades:
- **Basic Backpack** - Adds 9 extra slots (requires Workbench Tier 2)
- **Backpack Upgrade 1** - Additional slots (requires Workbench Tier 3)
- **Backpack Upgrade 2** - More slots (requires restoring 100 Memories)

:::tip Backpack Tips
Backpacks are consumable items that permanently unlock extra inventory slots for your character. Click the third diamond below the Crafting tab to view your backpack's contents.
:::

### Equipment Slots

Armor and equipment slots:
- **Helm** - Head protection
- **Cuirass** - Torso armor (highest defensive boost)
- **Gauntlets** - Hand protection
- **Greaves** - Leg armor
- **Shield** - Off-hand defensive equipment
- **Back gear** - Cape or back accessories

:::info Auto-Equip
If the corresponding armor slot is empty, new armor pieces will equip automatically; otherwise, they go into your inventory.
:::

## Crafting System

Hytale uses specialized workbenches for different types of crafting:

### Builder's Workbench

- Basic crafting recipes
- Building materials
- Tool creation
- Upgradeable to unlock more recipes

### Armorer's Workbench

- Helm, Cuirass, Gauntlets, Greaves crafting
- Shield creation
- Requires Copper Ingots, Tree Trunks, and Stone to craft

### Blacksmith's Anvil

- Weapon upgrades
- Equipment enhancement
- Item repair

### Chef's Stove

- Food preparation
- Cooking recipes
- Buff-granting dishes

### Farmer's Workbench

- Farming tools
- Seed processing
- Agricultural items

### Additional Stations

- **Salvager's Workbench** - Break down items for materials
- **Alchemist's Workbench** - Potions and magical items
- **Arcanist's Workbench** - Late-game magical crafting (requires Thorium and Essence of the Void)

## World Map

Press **M** for the full map.

### Features

- **Top-down view** - Birds-eye view of the world
- **Zoom** - Use scroll wheel to zoom in/out for precise marker placement
- **Your position** - Blue arrow showing your location and facing direction
- **Custom markers** - Right-click to place markers visible to your group

### Map Icons

| Icon | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Blue arrow | Your position and direction |
| Spawn icon | Your spawnpoint |
| Spiral icon | Forgotten Temple Gateway |
| Player icons | Other players in your lobby |
| Quest markers | Active objectives |
| Structure icons | Nearby buildings and dungeons |

## Settings

### Graphics

- Texture quality
- Render distance (minimum 192 blocks, recommended 348)
- Visual effects
- VSync and max FPS

### Audio

- Master volume
- Music / Effects / Voice
- Spatial audio

### Gameplay

- Camera preferences (first/third person default)
- Field of View (30-120, default 75)
- Help indicators

### Controls

- Full keybind customization
- Sprint/Crouch toggle vs hold behavior
- Mouse sensitivity (horizontal and vertical)
