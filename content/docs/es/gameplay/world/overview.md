# The World of Orbis

Orbis is the world of Hytale, a rich and diverse planet where your adventure takes place.

## Overview

Orbis is a procedurally generated world, meaning each game is unique. The world is divided into **Regions**, each with its own biomes, creatures, resources, and challenges.

### Features

- **Procedural generation** - Infinite and unique world
- **Verticality** - Deep caves and high mountains
- **Day/night cycle** - Affects creatures and gameplay
- **Dynamic weather** - Rain, snow, storms

## World Structure

### Zones

Orbis is divided into six elemental zones, with the first four available at launch:

| Zone | Name | Element | Difficulty |
|------|------|---------|------------|
| Zone 1 | Emerald Grove | Earth | Easy |
| Zone 2 | Howling Sands | Wind | Medium |
| Zone 3 | Borea | Water | Hard |
| Zone 4 | Devastated Lands | Fire | Very Hard |
| Zone 5 | Skylands (placeholder) | Lightning | TBD |
| Zone 6 | Poisonlands (placeholder) | Void | TBD |

:::info Starting Zone Selection
Players can choose to start in Zone 1, Zone 2, or Zone 3. Zone 4 is endgame content.
:::

### Regions

Regions are a newer subdivision system within zones. Each region represents a collection of 3 to 5 biomes with a cohesive theme, providing finer control over world identity.

| Region | Zone | Theme |
|--------|------|-------|
| Central Grasslands | Zone 1 | Open prairies, sparse forests, rocky outcrops |
| Emerald Wilds | Zone 1 | Dense forests, swamps, clearings |
| Wild Savannah | Zone 2 | Grassy savannas with sparse trees |
| Whisperfrost Frontier | Zone 3 | Taiga forests, frozen rivers |
| Earthglade | Zone 1 | Deep forests |

## World Generation

### World Generation V1 (Launch)

The system available at Early Access launch (January 2026):
- Already packed with playable biomes
- Content ready for exploration
- Structures and dungeons
- Procedurally generated terrain for each zone

### World Generation V2 (Future)

The complete world of Orbis is being rebuilt on V2:
- Superior visual and gameplay experience
- More varied and complex terrain features
- Enhanced biome diversity
- Accessible during development via **Gateways** (special portals)
- All biomes will be shared with the community for modding

:::tip Update
V2 will become the primary generator once completed. V1 worlds will remain accessible, though V1 will eventually stop generating new chunks.
:::

## Points of Interest (POI)

The world is dotted with structures and special locations:

### Natural Structures

- **Caves** - Underground systems with ores
- **Ravines** - Deep canyons
- **Waterfalls** - Picturesque water points
- **Rock formations** - Natural landmarks

### Built Structures

- **Villages** - Friendly NPCs, trading
- **Camps** - Enemies and loot
- **Towers** - Vertical challenges
- **Dungeons** - Underground adventures
- **Temples** - Lore and treasures

### Ruins

- **Ancient monuments** - World history
- **Abandoned fortresses** - Risky exploration
- **Battle sites** - Recoverable equipment

## Exploration

### Tips

1. **Mark your path** - Use the map
2. **Prepare yourself** - Food, weapons, torches
3. **Observe the environment** - Visual clues
4. **Progress in stages** - Don't venture too far too fast

### Rewards

Exploration yields:
- **Rare resources** - Materials exclusive to each region
- **Unique equipment** - Found in chests
- **Knowledge** - Lore and history
- **Experience** - Character progression

## The Sky and Space

Orbis features:
- **Two moons** - Affect tides and certain creatures
- **Constellations** - Night navigation
- **Auroras** - Phenomena in cold regions

## The Deep Ocean

The Deep Ocean technically stands separate from other zones and has been described by developers as "a zone all by itself." It separates the Infinite Lands from the main continent of Orbis. Tropical islands exist within the ocean.
