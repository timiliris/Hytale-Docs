# Hytale Content Creators

Welcome to the comprehensive directory of content creators covering Hytale! As the game launches on January 13, 2026, this community is rapidly growing. This page will be regularly updated as new creators emerge.

## The Hytale Creator Community

Hytale has attracted a passionate community of content creators even before its official release. From news coverage and speculation to tutorials and creative content, these creators help build and inform the community.

### Types of Content

- **News & Updates** - Coverage of official announcements and updates
- **Gameplay** - Let's plays, tutorials, and guides
- **Reviews & Analysis** - In-depth looks at game features
- **Creative Content** - Fan art, music, builds, and machinima
- **Modding** - Mod showcases and development tutorials
- **Streaming** - Live gameplay and community interaction

## How to Become a Hytale Creator

### Getting Started

1. **Choose Your Niche**
   - News and updates coverage
   - Gameplay and tutorials
   - Creative content (builds, art, music)
   - Modding and development
   - Streaming and entertainment

2. **Set Up Your Platform**
   - YouTube for video content
   - Twitch for live streaming
   - Twitter/X for community engagement
   - Discord for community building

3. **Create Quality Content**
   - Focus on value for viewers
   - Maintain consistent upload schedule
   - Engage with your audience
   - Collaborate with other creators

## Submit a Creator

### How to Add a Creator

Want to be featured on this list? Here's how:

1. **Requirements**
   - Active Hytale content (or commitment to create)
   - Established platform presence
   - Quality, original content
   - Positive community standing

2. **Submission Process**
   - [Contribute to the wiki](/docs/community/contributing)
   - Include: Name, Platform, Links, Content Type, Language
   - Provide proof of content creation

3. **Review Process**
   - Community review
   - Quality verification
   - Addition to appropriate category

### Criteria for Inclusion

- **Active Content** - Regular uploads/streams
- **Quality** - Well-produced content
- **Relevance** - Hytale-focused or includes Hytale
- **Community Standing** - Positive reputation

1. **DO**
   - Give credit to Hypixel Studios
   - Follow community guidelines
   - Create original commentary/content
   - Respect other creators

2. **DON'T**
   - Claim official affiliation without permission
   - Use assets against guidelines
   - Create misleading content
   - Harass other community members
